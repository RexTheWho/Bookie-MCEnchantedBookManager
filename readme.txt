HOW TO USE:

  Open Bookie.exe

  Drag and Drop a minecraft sprite into the window

  Click the book to save it

TECHNICAL:
  name1.png
    - Implies its enchanted with tier unique icons
    - Only applies to Level 1
  
  name.png
    - Implies its levels do not matter
    - Icon will be the same regardless of level
  
  ModId being filled out prefixes the enchantment ID
    - enchantmentIDs=yaddayadda
    - enchantmentIDs=mycoolmod:yaddayadda
